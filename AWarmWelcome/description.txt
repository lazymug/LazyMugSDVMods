[size=6][b]A Warm Welcome[/b][/size]

[i]Starting a new life in a strange town can be daunting — but not in Pelican Town![/i]

With [b]A Warm Welcome[/b], every villager greets your arrival with a personalized letter and a small gift to help you settle into your new farming life. Each note is written in the character's own voice, and each gift is something that villager personally loves.

[line]

[size=5][b]Features[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]A letter from everyone.[/b] Every villager sends you a personal welcome letter as you get to know the valley.
[*][b]Thoughtful, in-character gifts.[/b] Each gift is an item the villager loves. Fruits and vegetables are sent as seeds so you can grow them yourself.
[*][b]Personality-driven writing.[/b] Every letter reflects the sender's personality, quirks and speech.
[*][b]Sensible pacing.[/b] Romanceable villagers write once you've grown a little closer (2.5 hearts); everyone else writes over the first few weeks, spread out so your mailbox is never flooded.
[*][b]Fully translatable.[/b] Ships with English and Brazilian Portuguese (pt-BR).
[/list]

[line]

[size=5][b]Compatibility[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Stardew Valley Expanded[/b] — welcome letters for the SVE villagers.
[*][b]Ridgeside Village[/b] — welcome letters and loved gifts for the Ridgeside cast. [i](New in 1.3.0)[/i]
[*][b]East Scarp[/b] — welcome letters and loved gifts for the East Scarp cast. [i](New in 1.3.0)[/i]
[/list]
All of the above are optional — install any combination and A Warm Welcome adapts automatically.
