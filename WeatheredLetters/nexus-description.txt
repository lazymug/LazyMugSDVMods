[size=5][b]Weathered Letters[/b][/size]

[i]"The valley remembers. The stones remember."[/i]

[size=4][b]About[/b][/size]
Scattered across Stardew Valley, old weathered letters lie hidden — waiting to be found. Each one reveals a piece of your grandfather's life: the day he arrived at the farm, the friends he made, the hardships he endured, and the love he carried until the very end.

Discover [b]15 handwritten letters[/b] from Grandpa and other familiar faces, and uncover a story that spans all four seasons.

[size=4][b]How It Works[/b][/size]
Letters appear naturally as you play. Every few days, a new letter becomes available in one of five discovery methods:

[list]
[*][b]Farm[/b] — A mysterious note appears on the ground somewhere on your farm.
[*][b]Beach[/b] — Something washes ashore on the beach.
[*][b]Mines[/b] — A faded piece of paper is found deep underground.
[*][b]Trash Cans[/b] — A crumpled letter turns up while checking the garbage.
[*][b]Fishing[/b] — An old note is caught inside a treasure chest.
[/list]

When hints are enabled, you'll receive a subtle HUD message at the start of the day telling you where to look.

[size=4][b]The Letters[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]10 letters from Grandpa[/b] — His journey from city life to the valley, his friendships, his discoveries in the mines, and his reflections on love and legacy.
[*][b]1 letter from Lewis[/b] — A young mayor's heartfelt thanks.
[*][b]1 letter from Evelyn[/b] — A wife's plea about George, and the accident that changed everything.
[*][b]1 letter from Walt[/b] — The old fisherman who taught Grandpa patience.
[*][b]1 letter from ???[/b] — Something ancient. Something that remembers.
[*][b]1 final reward letter[/b] — Grandpa's last message to you, along with a very special gift.
[/list]

Letters are tied to seasons — spring letters appear in spring, summer in summer, and so on — but they'll eventually appear regardless if you play long enough.

[size=4][b]Collect Them All[/b][/size]
Track your progress as you find each letter. A HUD message shows your count ([i]"Weathered Letter found! (3/15)"[/i]), and once all 15 are collected, you'll receive a final letter from Grandpa in the mail — along with a [b]Prismatic Shard[/b] as his parting gift.

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]
Fully configurable via [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/stardewvalley/mods/5098]Generic Mod Config Menu[/url] (optional):

[list]
[*][b]Enable Mod[/b] — Toggle the mod on or off.
[*][b]Days Between Letters[/b] — Minimum days between letter discoveries (1-28, default: 7).
[*][b]Show Spawn Hints[/b] — Toggle HUD hints for where to look.
[/list]

[size=4][b]Console Commands[/b][/size]
For testing or screenshots:
[code]
wl_status          — Show collection progress
wl_spawn           — Force the next letter to spawn
wl_spawn_here [id] — Spawn a letter next to the player
wl_read list       — List all letter IDs
wl_read [id]       — Open a specific letter to read
[/code]
