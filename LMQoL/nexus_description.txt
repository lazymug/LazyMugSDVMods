[size=5][b]LM QoL[/b][/size]
A growing collection of small quality-of-life improvements for Stardew Valley, bundled into a single mod.

[line]

[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[size=3][b]Auto Gate[/b][/size]
Gates open automatically when you walk near them and close after you move away.

[list]
[*]Opens any gate within 1 tile of the player
[*]Closes automatically after a configurable delay (default: ~1 second)
[*]Works with all gate types
[*]No more fumbling with right-click while carrying items!
[/list]

[size=3][b]Sell Price Tooltip[/b][/size]
Hover over any raw item to see how much it's worth after processing.

[list]
[*]Shows prices for Keg, Preserves Jar, Oil Maker, and Smoker
[*]Supports Artisan profession bonus (+40%) — tagged with [A]
[*]Highlights the most profitable option with an asterisk (*)
[*]Covers special recipes: Wheat to Beer, Hops to Pale Ale, Coffee Bean to Coffee, and more
[/list]

[b]Example tooltip:[/b]
[code]
--- Processing ---
Keg: 2250g (Wine) [A] *
Preserves Jar: 1330g (Jelly) [A]
* = best profit
[/code]

[line]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]
All features can be toggled and customized individually through [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/stardewvalley/mods/5098]Generic Mod Config Menu[/url].

[list]
[*][b]Auto Gate[/b] — Enable/disable, adjust close delay (10–300 ticks)
[*][b]Sell Price Tooltip[/b] — Enable/disable, toggle Artisan prices, toggle best option highlight
[/list]

[line]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]
[list=1]
[*]Install [url=https://smapi.io]SMAPI[/url]
[*]Install [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/stardewvalley/mods/5098]Generic Mod Config Menu[/url] (optional, for in-game settings)
[*]Download this mod and extract the [b]LMQoL[/b] folder into your [b]Mods[/b] directory
[*]Launch the game through SMAPI
[/list]
